Stray is one of the first games I played on my Steamdeck when I got it. It was a perfect game for me at the time. It was quick, it was good, it was steam punk, and it had cats.

## Status

I beat the game while my then 14 year old cat slept beside me. I'm not ashamed to admit that I cried at the ending and startled my cat when I reached for her. Beautiful game and a beautiful story.
